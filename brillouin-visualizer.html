Solid State Physics Guide

Real Space & Lattice
A crystal is a repeating pattern of atoms in physical space (Real Space). The unit cell is the smallest repeating block. The lattice constant a defines the unit cell dimension in Ångströms (1 Å = 10⁻¹⁰ meters).
